Liyue the City of Contracts

Liyue is one of the seven nations in Teyvat. It is the city-state that worships Morax, the Geo Archon. A bountiful harbor that lies in the east of Teyvat. Mountains stand tall and proud alongside the stone forest, that, together with the open plains and lively rivers, make up Liyue's bountiful landscape, which shows its unique beauty through each of the four seasons. Just how many gifts from the Geo God lie in wait amongst the rocks of Liyue's mountains
