There are different playable characters within the game each categorized as a 4 star or 5 star. The difference between a 4 star and a 5 star is that 5 star characters are more efficient to use,it is only obtained on the gacha and luck decides whether you will get one or not. On the other hand 4 star characters are slightly easy to obtain but it is random unlike 5 star characters that are specifically featured that changes differently per version update. Just by doing the story quest you will be able to unlock the starter characters there will be 4 characters to be unlocked this way, the main character of the story which is the traveler a 5 star and the remaining 3 will be 4 star characters. while the rest are mainly obtained via gacha with the use of gacha currency in the game or by participating in certain events and obtain characters with the event rewards. Each charater comes from a specific nation from the story and weilds different power from the seven elements. Currently there are still no released dendro(nature) playable character weilding that element.

Each character has two unique combat skills: an elemental skill and an elemental burst. The elemental skill can be used at any time except for the cooldown period immediately after use. In contrast, the elemental burst has an energy cost, requiring the user to amass sufficient elemental energy by defeating enemies or inflicting elemental status effects. Characters have control over one of seven natural elements: Cryo, Dendro, Pyro, Hydro, Anemo, Electro and Geo, which correspond to ice, nature, fire, water, air, electricity, and earth respectively. Characters are assigned one type of the 5 types weapon to use namely bow, sword, claymore, catalyst, and polearm

Of course not all playable characters belong to a nation since the twins Aether and Lumine are not originally from that world, including a special colab character Aloy that is a crossover character from the game Horizon Zero Dawn to Genshin Impact.
