Items

Items are very important, there are many kinds of items to use in Genshin Impact. Such items can give you support and advantages throughout your entire adventure in Genshin Impact. Items can also be used to ascend weapons or characters and increase character talent level. Ascension increases the maximum level of a character or weapon. Some items can be farmed from enemies as materials. On the other hand talents level makes the character's skills more stronger with each level up. While other items can be gathered in the wild, it may be from cathcing or hunting a certain animal or it can be found growing in a specific location in the game. They can be either use as ingredients or materials.

Here are the categories and purposes of the items:

Guide   Items   Enemies
Character Development Items: Character Ascension Materials

EXP(Experience)Materials
These are items used to increase the level of a character. It can be obtained by opening ramdom chest or as rewards from quest and events.
Local Specialties
These are used to increase the maximum level of a character. Each character has each specific assigned local specialty from their nation. The twins are a separate case. It commonly grows in the wild so you just have to keep an eye on them when you are exploring.




Gem shards and Gems
These are used to increase the maximum level of a character. A specific element is assigned in each gem. The element of the gem must match the element of the character you intend to use it on. The twins have their own set of gems. The gem shards and gems are obtained by defeating a boss with a element corresponding to your desired shards or gems. It can also be obtain from weekly bosses.
Boss Drops
These are used to increase the maximum level of a character. Just like the local speciallty a specific boss drop is assigned to a character. Obviously obtained by defeating bosses.

Character Development Items: Character Talent Level Up Materials

Crown of Insight
Crown of Insight is used to level up a character's talent to its maximum level. It is only obtained in events.
Talent Books
It is another item required to level a character's talent and a specific book is assigned to a character. It can be obtained by completing domains(dungeons).



Weekly Boss Drops
Used to level up character talent. Quite similar to a regular boss the only difference is that it can only give rewards and drops once a week. In addition there is no need for element compatibility because a specific drop is assigned to a charater regardless of the element of the weekly boss.

Character Development Items: Weapon Ascension Materials

Enhancement Crystals
Similar to experience materials it is used to increase the weapon's level. Obtained by opening random chest, crafting, quest, and events.


Weapon Ascension Materials
Similar to talent books it is obtained by completing domains. but it is used to increase a weapon's maximum level rather than incresing its current level.

Character Development Items: Character and Weapon Ascension Materials

Character and Weapon Ascension Materials
Obtained from various monsters and enemies it is use for both character and weapons to increase their maximum level.

Items: Consumables

Foods
Foods are items that can aid you in your adventures. Foods can be cooked if you have the ingredients and your near a cooking spot. Provided that you have learned the recipe for that dish. Foods can heal you, revive fallen characters, and improved your stamina/attack/defense.
Potions
Similar to a food but it is crafted in an alchemy table. It does not require any recipe, you just need to have the right ingredients. It can provide resistance to elements and provide critical rate increase for the corresponding element it was made from.

Items: Gadgets

Gadgets
Gadgets have various uses and its commonly handy when exploring. It can be obtained as event rewards or can be crafted if you have the right blueprint for the desired gadget.
