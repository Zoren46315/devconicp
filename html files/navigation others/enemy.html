Enemies

Enemies are basically the hostile NPC(Non-Playable Characters) that the player must defeat if the quest requires it, for farming materials, events, etc. There different kinds of enemies which is shown in the tables below:

Guide   Items   Enemies
Common Enemies

Hilichurls
Hilichurls are the basic enemy in Genshin Impact. They're easy to battle but can be pretty annoying when in groups. They also have elemental variations aside the normal ones.

Samachurls
They are the shamans of the hilichurls. The only member of the hilichurl tribe that can cast a spell to aid their fellow hilichurls.

Slimes
Slimes are made up from the elements. They are easy to battle if the character you are using weilds an element that is effective againts the element that the slime id formed from.

Whopperflowers
They are similar to slimes but in flower form.

Elite Enemies

Mitachurls
The vanguards of the hilichurl tribe. Bigger than a usual hilichurl their strength is above average and can cause decent damage to your characters.

Ruin Guard(left) Ruin Hunter(right)
Remnants of a lost civilization. Must be approached carefully because these automatrons are very dangerous. A serious hit from their attacks can easily defeat a character. Strategic use of characters is the key on defeating this enemy.

The Abyss
Abyss mages are troublesome opponents. Their shields are pretty hard to deal with and as long as the have their shield is going to be a painful battle. But once the shields are destroyed they suddenly becomes easy preys.

Bosses

Oceanid(top-left) Geo Hyphostasis(top-right) Pyro Regisvine(bottom)
Bosses are hunted for the ascension materials they drop upon defeat. They can spawn again after defeat after a short period of time the moment they are defeated. That is why if the materials you need are still not enough you can still challenge them again.

Weekly Bosses

Stormterror(top) Andrius The Wolf of the North(bottom)
Weekly Bosses are the strongest in all the enemies. They are simply the concluding opponents of each major quest in Genshin Impact. They drop various items upon defeat and the said rewards can only be claimed once a week. But that doesn't mean that they can not be challenge again but this time upon defeat there will be no rewards.
