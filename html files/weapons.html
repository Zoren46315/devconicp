Weapons

Weapons are used by the characters to deal damage to enemies. There are 5 types of weapons in total; which are sword, claymore, bow, catalyst, and polearm. Each weapon has a unique usage i.e a bow and catalyst are effective against flying enemies and enables the player to attack from afar, the claymore can be used to break enemy shields and deal heavy damage, and the sword and polearm are good for agile close combats. The weapons are also rank from 1 star to 5 star. 1 star to 3 star weapons can be obtained by opening chest in the game, while some are obtained by using the gacha. A number of 4 star weapons can be crafted in the blacksmith while the rest are obtained through gacha including the 5 star weapons. Just as same in the characters the 4 star and 5 star weapons are quite similar to the 4 and 5 star characters when it comes to gacha mechanics and ascending and leveling them up.

Artifacts are another type of equipment that characters use to boost their stats. Each artifact provides a primary stat and a secondary stat, the number of secondary stat differs depending on the artifact star that also ranges from 1-5. As for the stats provided by each artifact it is all ramdom of the following:

  • HP-Health Points
  • ATK-Attack
  • DEF-Defense
  • Energy Recharge
  • Elemental Mastery
  • CRIT DMG- Critical Damage
  • CRIT RATE- Critical Rate
  • Healing Bonus
  • Physical Bonus
  • Elemental Bonus
