Banners

Home   Story   Characters   Weapons and Artifacts   Banners   Others

Banners also known as the gacha and wishing, are a part of the game where you have to spend the in game currency you've earned to get a new weapon or character. But you can also spend real money to buy the in game currency to wish on the banners. The gacha usually have three banners in total. The first two are the featured banners, each designated for a new character or weapon. In some cases if there is no new character to be release in the next version, they will have a rerun where players who didn't get the chance to get that weapon or character the first time they have been released. The gacha have a pity system that guarantees a 4 star character or weapon on the 10th time you pull(wished) in the banner if you did not get any on the first 9 attempts. If on the other hand, you failed to get the featured 5 star of the banner in 89 wishes the 90th wish is a guaranteed 5 star, if it happened to a 5 star that is not featured then the next 5 star is guaranteed to be the featured character or weapon. As for the third banner it is a permanent banner featuring ten kinds of 5 star weapons and five 5 star characters. It is not a popular banner because a total of fifteen 5 star will set the odds of getting what you want among the fifteen 5 star quite difficult.

Other Details about the gacha will be presented in a table below.

Currencies used for wishing

Acquaint Fate
Acquaint Fate is the currency used to wish on the permanent banner and the additional beginner's banner at the start of the game.
Interwined Fate
Interwined Fate is the currency used to wish on the banners featuring a new or rerun 5 star character and weapons.

Primogems
Primogems it is a substitute for both Acquaint and Interwined Fate. 160 Primogems is equals to 1 of the two Fates it can be either converted into a specific Fates or used as it is.
Genesis Crystals
Genesis Crystals are needed to be purchased by the player. It can be converted in to Primogems with 1 Genesis Crystal is equal to 1 Primogems.

Other Kinds of Banners

Beginner's Banner
You can only wish 20 times in this banner and it will be gone afterwards. This banner gives you a guaranteed Noelle and you get 20% discount when pulling 10 times at once
Pernament Banner
As the name implies this banner is permanent and never changes its featured 5 stars but it adds a new item to the possible prizes if there is a new 4 star character that is been released.
